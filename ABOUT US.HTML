About Us

The Rwanda National Police (RNP) is committed to maintaining law and order, ensuring public safety, and promoting peace and security in Rwanda. Established in 2000, the RNP has evolved into a modern and community-oriented police force.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide quality policing services to all citizens, fostering trust and collaboration with the communities we serve. We aim to enhance the safety and security of individuals and property through proactive crime prevention and effective response strategies.

Core Activities

Crime Prevention and Investigation, Traffic Management and Road Safety, Community Policing Initiatives, Public Awareness Campaigns, Emergency Response Services.

Our Vision

To be a leading police force in Africa, recognized for its professionalism, innovation, and dedication to service excellence.
